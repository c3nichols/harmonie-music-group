Concerts and Events

 

Attending our Concerts

  

Anyone is very welcome to attend our concerts, our programme of music will vary between concerts and will likely include musicals, films, popular modern and classical pieces. There is usually something for everyone!

We always have an interval with tea, cake and a raffle.

Tickets can be purchased from any member of the band/choir, on the door or you can contact us directly from the website and our social media pages!
