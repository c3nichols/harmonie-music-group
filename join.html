Concert Band

We are always looking for new players to come and join the band so if you are tempted come on and give us a go! We play a range of music including popular musicals like Lion King, Greatest Showman, Les Miserables, pieces from films James Bond, Pink Panther, Stars Wars as well as some more classical. We are especially on the look out for more trumpets, horns, flutes and oboes but really any wind or percussion player is welcome! There is a range of playing standard across the sections, so if you are unsure of your ability then come and along and find out for yourself. Rehearsals take place weekly in Horam Thursday's 6:45pm to 8:15pm. You will need to bring your own instrument and music stand. If you are interested please contact us!

Voices in Harmonie

Voices in Harmonie is a pop up choir which means we get together for a few weeks in the lead up to a performance. We rehearse in Hellingly Village Hall Friday's 7:30pm to 9:00pm. A number of our members are also involved with the concert band but we also have others who come along because they just love to sing! The wonderful thing about singing is that we all have the ability so whether you have sung in a choir before or not it really doesn't matter just come along and give it a try. We usually sing in 4-part harmony accompanied by piano. Being able to read music is an advantage but not essential. If you would like to find out more please follow us and check the events section on our Facebook page or send us a message!
