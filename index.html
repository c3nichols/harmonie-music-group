Harmonie Concert Band

The Harmonie Concert Band, established in 2007, is a blend of woodwind, brass and percussion. A group of musicians with a mission to play good music and have fun. Rehearsals are in Horam, East Sussex on Thursdays between 6.30 and 8.00pm. Our players are of all ages and abilities. We give concerts, and perform at fetes and other events around East Sussex.

Voices of Harmonie Choir

Voices in Harmonie Choir was set up in 2014 as a pop up choir, we get together for weekly rehearsals in blocks of 6-weeks three times a year and work towards a performance. The choir ranges in abilities so whether you have singing experience or are coming along to sing with others for the first time you would be very welcome. We enjoy ourselves at rehearsals as well as focusing when needed! Our rehearsals take place on Friday evenings, 7.30-9.00pm, in Hellingly Village Hall.

Harmonie Musical Director

Danielle set up the Harmonie Concert Band and Voices in Harmonie community choir alongside her mum Frances. Danielle is also a private music teacher with over 20 years experience in teaching one-to-one music lessons for adults and children from age 6/7. Danielle's enthusiasm and love of music come across in the encouraging way she leads the Concert Band and Choir encrouaging all ages and abilities to get involved and share her love of making music!
